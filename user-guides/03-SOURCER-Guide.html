Roof HR - Sourcer User Guide

Your guide to recruiting and candidate management

Welcome, Sourcer!

As a Sourcer, your main focus is recruiting. You have access to candidates, the AI assistant, your personal portal, and time off requests. This guide will help you navigate the system efficiently.

Your Main Job: Recruiting!

The recruiting section is your home base. Most of your work will happen there.

1. Logging In

1 Go to roofhr.up.railway.app
2 Enter your @theroofdocs.com email address
3 Enter your password and click "Sign In"

2. Your Navigation Menu

Your sidebar is streamlined for recruiting work:

My Portal - Your personal dashboard
Susan AI - AI assistant for questions
Recruiting - Candidates & Analytics (YOUR MAIN AREA)
Time Off - Request PTO
Focused Access: You won't see Dashboard, Documents, Employees, Facilities, Reviews, or Settings. This keeps your interface clean and focused on recruiting.

3. Recruiting - Your Main Workspace

Viewing Candidates

1 Click "Recruiting" in the sidebar
2 Click "Candidates" to see your candidate pipeline
3 Use the search bar to find specific candidates
4 Filter by status (Applied, Screening, Interview, Offer, etc.)
[Screenshot: Candidate Pipeline]
Shows candidate cards organized by status columns

Working with a Candidate

1 Click on any candidate card to open their profile
2 View their resume, contact info, and application details
3 Add notes about your conversations
4 Update their status as they progress
5 Schedule interviews if needed
Tip: Always add notes after every candidate interaction. This helps the whole team stay informed!
[Screenshot: Candidate Detail View]
Shows candidate profile with notes, status, and action buttons

Moving Candidates Through Stages

1 Open the candidate's profile
2 Find the "Status" dropdown
3 Select the new stage:
  • APPLIED - Just submitted application
  • SCREENING - Initial review/phone screen
  • INTERVIEW - In-person/video interview stage
  • OFFER - Offer extended
  • HIRED - Accepted and starting
  • REJECTED - Not moving forward
4 Add a note explaining why you moved them

Recruiting Analytics

1 Click "Recruiting" > "Analytics"
2 View key metrics:
  • Total candidates in pipeline
  • Candidates by stage
  • Time to hire
  • Source effectiveness
[Screenshot: Recruiting Analytics Dashboard]
Shows charts and metrics for recruiting performance

4. Susan AI - Your Assistant

1 Click "Susan AI" in the sidebar
2 Type your question in the chat box
3 Get instant answers about:
  • Company policies
  • HR procedures
  • Benefits questions
  • General work questions
Tip: Susan is great for quick answers! Ask things like "What's our PTO policy?" or "How do I submit an expense report?"

5. Time Off Requests

1 Click "Time Off" > "PTO Requests"
2 Click "Request Time Off" button
3 Select your dates and type of time off
4 Add any notes and submit
5 Track your request status on the same page
[Screenshot: PTO Request Form]
Shows date picker and request submission form

6. My Portal

1 Click "My Portal" in the sidebar
2 View your personal information
3 See your documents and contracts
4 Update your profile if needed
