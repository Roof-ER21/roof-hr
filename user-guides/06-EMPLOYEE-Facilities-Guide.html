Roof HR - Employee Guide

With Special Facilities Access

Welcome, Alex!

You have standard employee access PLUS special access to the Facilities section for managing attendance and meeting rooms. This guide covers everything you can do.

1. Logging In

1 Go to roofhr.up.railway.app
2 Enter your @theroofdocs.com email
3 Enter your password and click "Sign In"

2. Your Navigation Menu

You have access to employee features PLUS Facilities:

My Portal - Your personal dashboard
Susan AI - AI assistant for questions
Facilities - Attendance, Meeting Rooms (SPECIAL ACCESS)
Time Off - Request PTO

3. Facilities - Your Special Access

Attendance Tracking

1 Click "Facilities" in the sidebar
2 Click "Attendance"
3 View employee check-ins and time tracking
4 Filter by date, department, or employee
[Screenshot: Attendance Dashboard]
Shows attendance records with check-in/out times

Meeting Rooms

1 Click "Facilities" > "Meeting Rooms"
2 View all available meeting rooms
3 Check room availability
4 Book a room for meetings
[Screenshot: Meeting Rooms List]
Shows rooms with capacity and availability status

Room Calendar

1 Click "Facilities" > "Room Calendar"
2 View calendar with all room bookings
3 Click on a time slot to make a booking
4 Fill in meeting details and save
[Screenshot: Room Calendar View]
Shows weekly/daily calendar with room bookings color-coded

4. Standard Employee Features

My Portal

1 Click "My Portal" to view your info

Susan AI

1 Click "Susan AI" > "AI Assistant"
2 Ask questions about policies, procedures, etc.

Time Off

1 Click "Time Off" > "PTO Requests"
2 Submit and track your PTO requests
