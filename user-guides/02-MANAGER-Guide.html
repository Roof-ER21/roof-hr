Roof HR - Manager User Guide

Your guide to team management features

Welcome, Manager!

As a Manager, you have access to manage your team, view the employee directory, handle recruiting, and manage your team's documents and contracts. Here's everything you need to know.

1. Logging In

1 Go to roofhr.up.railway.app
2 Enter your @theroofdocs.com email address
3 Enter your password and click "Sign In"

2. Your Navigation Menu

Here's what you'll see in your sidebar:

Dashboard - Overview of team metrics
My Portal - Your personal employee portal
Team Dashboard - Your team's activities
Susan AI - AI assistant for questions
Recruiting - Candidates and Analytics
Documents - Your docs, Tools, Contracts
Employees - Employee Directory (view only)
Time Off - PTO Requests

What You WON'T See:

Facilities (Attendance, Meeting Rooms)
COI Tracking
Reviews

3. Your Key Features

Dashboard

1 Click "Dashboard" to see team overview
2 View key metrics about your team

Employee Directory (View Only)

1 Click "Employees" > "Employee Directory"
2 Search for employees by name, email, or position
3 View employee details (role, department, hire date, status)
Note: You can VIEW all employees but cannot Add, Edit, or Delete. Contact HR if you need to make changes.
[Screenshot: Employee Directory - Manager View]
Shows employee list without Add/Edit/Delete buttons

Recruiting

1 Click "Recruiting" > "Candidates"
2 View all candidates in the hiring pipeline
3 Click on a candidate to see their full profile
4 Update candidate status and add notes
5 Go to "Analytics" to see recruiting metrics
[Screenshot: Recruiting Candidates View]
Shows candidate pipeline with status columns

Documents (Your Docs + Company Docs)

1 Click "Documents" > "All Documents"
2 View documents you created and company-wide documents
3 Upload new documents for your team
Note: You can only see your own documents and documents shared with all employees. You won't see other managers' private documents.

Contracts (Your Team Only)

1 Click "Documents" > "Contracts"
2 View contracts for yourself and your direct reports
3 Create new contracts for your team members
4 Track contract status (Draft, Sent, Signed)
Note: You can only see contracts you created or contracts for employees who report directly to you.

Time Off Requests

1 Click "Time Off" > "PTO Requests"
2 View PTO requests
3 Submit your own PTO requests
Tip: PTO approval is handled by Ahmed, Ford, Reese, and Oliver. You can view requests but not approve them.

Susan AI

1 Click "Susan AI" > "AI Assistant"
2 Ask Susan questions about HR policies, procedures, etc.
3 Get instant answers without waiting for HR
