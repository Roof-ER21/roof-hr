Roof HR - HR Admin User Guide

Your guide to HR administration and recruiting

Welcome, HR Admin!

As an HR Admin, you have full access to the HR system including recruiting, employee management, documents, contracts, and more. This guide covers your key responsibilities.

Your Primary Focus Areas:

  • Recruiting - Managing the candidate pipeline
  • Onboarding - Setting up new hires
  • Contracts - Creating and sending employment agreements
  • Employee Records - Maintaining employee information

1. Logging In

1 Go to roofhr.up.railway.app
2 Enter your @theroofdocs.com email
3 Enter your password and click "Sign In"

3. Recruiting Workflow

Managing the Candidate Pipeline

1 Go to Recruiting > Candidates
2 View all candidates organized by status
3 Click a candidate to view their full profile
4 Update status, add notes, schedule interviews

Moving Candidates to Hired

1 Open candidate profile
2 Change status to "OFFER"
3 Send contract (see Contracts section below)
4 Once signed, change status to "HIRED"
5 Create employee record in Employee Directory

4. Contracts - Creating & Sending

Create a New Contract

1 Go to Documents > Contracts
2 Click "New Contract"
3 Select a Template (Employment, NDA, Contractor, etc.)
4 Choose Recipient Type: Employee or Candidate
5 Select the person from the dropdown
6 Review and customize the contract content
7 Click "Save as Draft" or "Send for Signature"
Tip: Sending triggers an email notification to the recipient with a link to sign.

Track Contract Status

1 Go to Documents > Contracts
2 View status column:
  • DRAFT - Not sent yet
  • SENT - Waiting for signature
  • VIEWED - Recipient opened it
  • SIGNED - Complete!
  • REJECTED - Recipient declined

5. Employee Onboarding

Add a New Employee

1 Go to Employees > Employee Directory
2 Click "Add Employee" (top right)
3 Fill in employee details (name, email, role, department, etc.)
4 Click "Create Employee"

Onboarding Checklist

1 Go to Employees > Onboarding Checklist
2 Create/assign onboarding tasks to new hire
3 Track completion status

6. Document Management

Upload Documents

1 Go to Documents > All Documents
2 Click "Upload Document"
3 Select file and set visibility (Public, Employee, Manager, Admin)
4 Click "Upload"

COI Tracking

1 Go to Documents > COI Tracking
2 View all certificates of insurance
3 Track expiration dates
4 Upload new COIs as needed

7. PTO Management

View PTO Requests

1 Go to Time Off > PTO Requests
2 View all pending, approved, and denied requests

Manage PTO Policies

1 Go to Time Off > PTO Policies
2 Configure accrual rates, max balances, etc.
